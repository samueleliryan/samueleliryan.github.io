About Me

Bright beginnings in tech

From the Beginning

I've always been interested in technology. From the beginning, my Dad started exposing me to great technologies, from keeping me updated on space travel to medical advancements, and I knew I wanted to make a difference too.

I started thinking I would be an inventor, making world changing inventions and fixing problems near me, but now I have the bigger picture. I want to become an engineer, creating and innovating new products and systems to fix even the worlds biggest problems, and learning to code is one of the first few steps I have taken toward this.

The Nextech Experience

Working at Nextech I believe changed my life. Before I went into it I thought computer coding was next to impossible, that small businesses were nearly a myth, and that I wouldn't use this stuff past highschool. And I was completeley wrong. Now that I have gone through the program, I know the basics of coding and could tackle projects with the knowledge I have. I now plan to start my own small business right out of college, and I will use the skills I learned and connections I made this summer for the rest of my life.
